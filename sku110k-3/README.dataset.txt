The SKU-110K dataset collects 11,762 densely packed shelf images from thousands of supermarkets around the world, including locations in the United States, Europe, and East Asia. The dataset can be downloaded from [here](http://trax-geometry.s3.amazonaws.com/cvpr_challenge/SKU110K_fixed.tar.gz) or [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1iq93lCdhaPUN0fWbLieMtzfB1850pKwd) and is provided solely for academic and non-commercial purposes.
